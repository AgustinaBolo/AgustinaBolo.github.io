About Me

Welcome! I'm Agustina. I am currently in my final year of the Systems Analyst Developer Degree at the Technological University of Argentina.

As someone who is tech-savvy and adaptable, I quickly learn and incorporate new software into my work. I also make use of various programs and AI tools to ensure the best possible results for every project.
